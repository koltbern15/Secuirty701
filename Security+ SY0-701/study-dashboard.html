Exam-day tactics

  • Do PBQs first, then flag-skip-return on hard items — don't burn time early.
  • Watch qualifiers: NOT EXCEPT LEAST — they flip the question.
  • Pick the BEST answer, not just a correct-sounding one.
  • "First step?" usually = contain / verify / document.
  • Never leave a question blank — no penalty for guessing.
